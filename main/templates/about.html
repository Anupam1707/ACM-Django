About ACM NMIMS Indore

ACM NMIMS Indore Student Chapter

The ACM NMIMS Indore Student Chapter is a vibrant community of students passionate about computing. We are dedicated to bringing the vast resources and opportunities of the global ACM community to our campus. Our chapter focuses on three core pillars: community, innovation, and learning. We organize a variety of events, including CodeFests, Tech Workshop Series, Hackathons, and AI/ML Symposiums, to provide students with hands-on experience and networking opportunities. Our goal is to create a dynamic environment where students can learn, innovate, and grow as future leaders in technology.

About ACM Global

Publications & Conferences

ACM is a leading publisher of computing literature, including journals, magazines, and conference proceedings. It also hosts numerous conferences and supports 38 Special Interest Groups (SIGs) in various fields of computing.

Awards & Recognition

ACM presents several prestigious awards for technical and professional achievements, including the A.M. Turing Award, often called the "Nobel Prize of computer science," which includes a $1 million prize.

Career Development & Networking

ACM provides resources for career development, including scholarships and job listings, and supports over 680 student chapters worldwide to enhance learning and professional networking.

What is ACM?

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is the world's largest educational and scientific computing society, uniting educators, researchers, and professionals to inspire dialogue, share resources, and address the field's challenges. As a global leader, ACM is dedicated to advancing computing as a science and a profession.

Membership Benefits

ACM membership offers a wealth of benefits, including access to the ACM Digital Library, which contains a vast collection of articles and publications. Members also get opportunities for networking, professional development, and participation in Special Interest Groups (SIGs). ACM's commitment to diversity and inclusion is reflected in its support for initiatives like ACM-W (Council on Women in Computing).

Education & Career Development

ACM is committed to fostering the next generation of computing professionals. It provides a wide range of educational resources, including online courses, webinars, and the ACM Learning Center. For career development, ACM offers a job board, career counseling, and mentorship programs. Student members can join local chapters to connect with peers and professionals.

Advocacy & Public Policy

ACM plays a crucial role in shaping public policy related to computing and technology. Through its Public Policy Council, ACM provides non-partisan, technically-informed advice to policymakers on issues such as cybersecurity, data privacy, and artificial intelligence. This work helps ensure that technology is used in a way that benefits society.
